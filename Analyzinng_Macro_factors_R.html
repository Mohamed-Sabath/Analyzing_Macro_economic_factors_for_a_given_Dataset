hypothesis = is there any relationship between Export and index and the Bisiness Investment.

Null hypothesis(H0) = There is no relationship between Export and index and the Bisiness Investment. Alternate Hypothesis (H1) = There is a relationship between Export and index and the Bisiness Investment.
